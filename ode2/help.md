<h2>2D Vector Field Grapher</h2>

<p>A tool to visualize first-order ordinary differential equations with up to 2 independent variables.Working in progress.</p>

<p>To plot the solution of ODE with $2$ variables, in the input box, assign $\mathrm{d}x/\mathrm{d}t$ to <code>x_t</code> and $\mathrm{d}y/\mathrm{d}t$ to <code>y_t</code>.</p>

<p>To plot the solution of ODE $\mathrm{d}y/\mathrm{d}x=f(x,y)$, assign $f(x,y)$ to <code>y_x</code>.</p>

<p>Solutions are shown around mouse cursor. Click to freeze solution. Right click to clear solution stack.</p>
